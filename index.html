Hello, I'm Tyler Machado and I'm a news developer.

I'm a journalist-turned-web-developer from Boston. I like to build things on the web for information, for art and for fun. I care about responsive design, good performance, inclusivity, and the DIY ethic.

Since March 2014 I've worked as Harvard Business Review's first editorial developer. Before that I lived in Burlington, Vermont, where I was the digital media manager for the newsweekly Seven Days.

Current toolkit: Lots of Javascript and CSS, some Node.js, a little dab of Python.
